Navier-Stokes Equations Research

Project Overview

This project was completed in MATH 0413 (Intro to Theoretical Mathematics) at the University of Pittsburgh. The paper illustrates extensive research on the Navier-Stokes equations, which describe the motion of fluids. The paper explains its derivation, as well as uses and the importance of the equations, showing strong computational and theoretical mathematical knowledge.

Objectives and Methodologies

As stated, the goal of the paper was to derive and illustrate the uses of the Navier Stokes equations. Research was conducted into the foundation of the equations, which specifically stem from Newton's Laws of Motion and basics of thermodynamics. This paper was also my introduction to Latex, which has proven to be a pivotal part of my mathematical writing career.
